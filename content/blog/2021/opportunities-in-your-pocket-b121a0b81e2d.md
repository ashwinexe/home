It wasn't until a year after I joined college I was blown by the fact everything I needed was in my pocket this whole time!

### Chapter 1: Hackathons

![](/blog/images/hashnode/opportunities-in-your-pocket-b121a0b81e2d-d88eae2df3.png)

Hackathons are one of the best ways to learn any tech in a short span of time. This may not mean you'll get mastery over the stack, but a good understanding of it.

So next time you want to learn a tech stack you fear, try using it in a hackathon instead. You'll get comfy with it by the end of the event.

Some resources:

- [Major League Hacking](http://mlh.io/)
- [Devpost](https://devpost.com/)
- [Devfolio](https://devfolio.co/)

### Chapter 2: Communities

If you've participated in a hackathon, chances are you'll find loads of communities to join. You can join any, or many. Joining a community that has a similar mindset or goals as you will boost your growth by many folds. Don't believe me? Just 2 months after I joined a few developer communities online, I had participated in 10 hackathons, made 5+ great projects, and won 2 hackathons, including my first international win.

This would normally take me a year if it weren't for the helpful community. That's right, everyone is ready to help you in the community.

Some great communities I am in:

- Hackerabad, my own community
- [MLH](https://discord.mlh.io/)
- Community Classroom: https://discord.gg/abf3MxdvD6
- [EddieHub](http://discord.eddiehub.org/)
- BlahajGang: https://blahaj.lol/discord
- MichiSpotlight: https://discord.gg/jvVScbtnS7
- OpInCo: https://discord.gg/WSuQKtH4
- Hack This Fall: https://discord.hackthisfall.tech/

### Chapter 3: Power of Social Media

![](/blog/images/hashnode/opportunities-in-your-pocket-b121a0b81e2d-98bda7d3c2.jpg)

I never thought social media could make me more productive than eating up my screen time. Committing your tasks in public, also known as learning in public, is a great way to maintain consistency in work towards your goal. You can't cheat when everyone's watching you.

Potential platforms:

- Twitter
- Linkedin
- Discord
- Hashnode
- Polywork

The best part about all of this is that they are free and you can get them from the same device you're reading this from.
