# Hello World! 👋

Welcome to my new personal website! I've been wanting to build a proper home on the internet for a while, and here it is.

I'll be writing about:

- **Tech & Community** - Insights from building developer communities
- **Side Projects** - Things I'm building and experimenting with
- **Life Updates** - General thoughts and learnings

## The Stack

This site is built with:

- Next.js 14 (App Router)
- Tailwind CSS
- Framer Motion
- MDX for blog posts

The design is inspired by [Teenage Engineering](https://teenage.engineering) and [bento.me](https://bento.me).
